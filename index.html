About Me

I am Dr.-Ing. Serhat Ilbey, a medical imaging specialist with expertise in Magnetic Particle Imaging (MPI) and Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI). I work on developing pulse sequences, and fast and robust calibration and reconstruction methods, focusing on sequence programming and computational imaging.
