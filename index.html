About Me

I am Serhat, a medical imaging specialist with expertise in:

  • Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI)
  • Magnetic Particle Imaging (MPI)

With a rich background in MRI and MPI research, including roles at Bruker BioSpin, University Medical Center Freiburg, and ASELSAN Research Center, I bring extensive experience in compressed sensing, sequence development, and advanced image reconstruction techniques. Dedicated to pushing the boundaries of medical imaging, I consistently aim to deliver precise, innovative solutions for complex challenges in the field.
